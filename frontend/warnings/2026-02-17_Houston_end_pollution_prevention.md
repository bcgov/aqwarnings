The Ministry of Environment and Parks in collaboration with the Northern
Health Authority has ended the Pollution Prevention Notice and open
burning restrictions within `<location>`{=html} that were issued on
February 16, 2026.

Local air quality has improved due to changing meteorological
conditions.

## More information
