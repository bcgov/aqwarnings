The Ministry of Environment and Parks in collaboration with the
Vancouver Island Health Authority has ended the Pollution Prevention
Notice and open burning restrictions within This is a TEST that were
issued on February 18, 2026.

This is a TEST.

## More information

{{< accordion_start title="Current conditions" initiallyOpen="true" >}}

The provincial air quality objective for PM~2.5~ is 25 micrograms per
cubic metre (μg/m^3^) averaged over 24 hours. 24-hour average PM~2.5~
concentrations are summarized below for Courtenay and nearest monitored
communities at 10:00 PM local time today:

::: {.table-responsive}

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Community                Courtenay   Duncan   Campbell River  Port Alberni
  ------------------------ ----------- -------- --------------- -------------
  24-hr average (μg/m^3^)  5.6         8.8      4.9             7.9

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

:::

Visit the provincial [air quality data
webpage](https://www2.gov.bc.ca/gov/content/environment/air-land-water/air/air-quality)
